## Other Anti-Patterns to Avoid

Beyond the centered hero, here are other overused SaaS patterns to skip:

❌ **"Trusted by" logo soup** (unless you have actual impressive logos)
❌ **"Meet our AI assistant" with a chat bubble mascot**
❌ **Feature grid with icons** (Settings icon, Shield icon, Rocket icon)
❌ **"See how [Company] increased X by 400%"** testimonial cards
❌ **Pricing tables** as the second section (move it down or to separate page)
❌ **"Join 10,000+ companies"** without proof
❌ **Generic stock photos** of people pointing at computers

✅ **Instead, consider:**
- Real screenshots of the actual product
- Live/real data where possible
- Specific numbers (not "thousands of traders" but "1,247 traders")
- Terminal/code aesthetic throughout
- Minimal text, maximum demonstration
